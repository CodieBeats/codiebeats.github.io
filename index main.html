Who is CodieBeats?
CB
CodieBeats
Independent Music Producer & Sound Designer · Philippines 🇵🇭
CodieBeats is a self-taught independent music producer from the Philippines who has been crafting original instrumentals and beat productions since 2016. His music blends raw emotion with hard-hitting rhythms — every beat is built to make you feel something before a single word is rapped or sung over it.

Known for his dark, moody, and melodic sound design, Codie produces music across multiple genres — from chest-pounding Trap and Drill to soulful R&B, ambient Lo-Fi, cinematic soundscapes, and groovy Afrobeats. His beats have been used by rappers, singers, YouTubers, and content creators worldwide.
