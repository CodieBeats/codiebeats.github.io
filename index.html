Pro Tips

The Rules That Separate Growing Channels from Dead Ones

📌 Title SEO is Non-Negotiable

Always start your beat video title with the searchable term: "Emotional Drake Type Beat 2026 – 'Lost'" — NOT "Lost – New Beat by CodieBeats." People search for the artist/mood, not your name (yet).

🖼️ Thumbnails Drive 50% of Your Clicks

Dark background + colored waveform + mood word = winning formula. Consistency matters more than perfection. Keep the same visual style so people recognize you in the feed.

⏰ Post Timing Matters

Best times for Filipino audiences + global reach: Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9pm Philippine time. Shorts can be posted any time — the algorithm surfaces them 24/7 regardless of upload time.

💬 Engagement = Algorithm Fuel

Reply to every comment in the first 2 hours after posting. Ask a question in every video description ("Drop a 🔥 if you'd use this beat"). Comments and replies signal to YouTube that your content is alive.

🔁 Repurpose Everything

One long-form video = 3 Shorts + 5 TikToks + 5 IG Reels + 3 Facebook posts + 1 community post. You're not making more content — you're slicing the same content for every platform.

📈 Consistency > Perfection

A mediocre beat posted consistently beats a perfect beat posted once a month every time. The algorithm rewards channels that upload regularly. Show up, even when the content isn't your best.
